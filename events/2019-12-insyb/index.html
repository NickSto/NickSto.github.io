Galaxy @ International Symposium on Bioinformatics 2019 (InSyB 2019)

Supported by ApBioNet, BioClues, ISCB, and Galaxy Australia

Contact: Sonika Tyagi, Gareth Price, Harpreet Singh

21 December 2019

The International Symposum on Bioinformatics 2019 (InSyB2019) will be held December 21-22, 2019 at Hans Raj Mahila Maha Vidyalaya, Jalandhar, Punjab, India.

This is a collaborative effort between APBioNET and one of the India's leading womens' colleges, Hans Raj Mahila Maha Vidyalaya (HMV), Jalandhar located in North of India in Punjab State. The conference is also supported by BioClues, ISCB, and Galaxy Australia.

The abstract deadline is 19th December.

Galaxy Australia @ InSyb 2019

Galaxy Australia will be featured in a workshop and a talk:

Date Time Topic Contact
22 December 10:30 Plenary: Galaxy Australia - lessons learnt from running a nationally resourced service Gareth Price
22 December 13:30 NGS Data Analysis using GALAXY Platform Workshop Sonika Tyagi

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NSF, NHGRI, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
