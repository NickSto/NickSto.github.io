Plant & Animal Genome XXVI (PAG 2018)

Galaxy is for Plants and Animals too...

  • Date: January 13 - 17, 2018
  • Location: San Diego, California
  • Contact: Presenters

Galaxy was at Plant and Animal Genome XXVI (PAG 2018), in San Diego, California, United States, January 13-17. There were also pre-conference events this year as well.
