3rd Galaxy High-Throughput-Sequencing (HTS) data analysis workshop

at Uni Freiburg

  • Date: 13 February 2017 - 17 February 2017
  • Location: Freiburg, DE
  • Contact: Freiburg Galaxy Team

See (external) blog entry

1-week full-day hands-on workshop in Freiburg. Topics: Galaxy Introduction, data analysis of: RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, Exome-seq, MethylC-seq.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NSF, NHGRI, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
