Galaxy @ ASM CUE

Teaching genomics to undergrads

  • Date: 1 August 2019 - 4 August 2019
  • Location: ASMCUE 2019, Tyson, Virginia, United States
  • Contact: Mohammad Heydarian, Anton Nekrutenko

Mo Heydarian of the Galaxy Project will present two sessions at the 2019 ASM Conference for Undergraduate Educators (ASMCUE)

Day Time Topic Where
Friday 8am Teaching undergraduate genomics with Galaxy (slides) Great Falls Room
Friday 9am Resources and recommendations for teaching undergraduate genomics with Galaxy (slides) Great Falls Room

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NSF, NHGRI, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
