Galaxy @ Plant & Animal Genome XXVII (PAG 2019)

Galaxy is for Plants and Animals too...

Contact: Presenters

12 January 2019

Galaxy will be at Plant and Animal Genome XXVII (PAG 20189, in San Diego, California, United States, January 12-16. This includes a Galaxy Workshop as well as other talks and posters featuring Galaxy. Here's what we know about:
