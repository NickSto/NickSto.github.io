PAG 2017

Galaxy is for Plants and Animals too...

  • Date: January 14 - 18, 2017
  • Location: San Diego, California
  • Contact: See web page

Galaxy will have a strong presence at Plant and Animal Genome XXIV (PAG 2017), being held in San Diego, California, United States, January 14-18. There is something (and often many things) every day of the conference.
