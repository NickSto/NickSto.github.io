2019 Galaxy Community Conference (GCC2019)

Freiburg, Germany, 1-6 July 2019

Conference book

#usegalaxy / chat

GCC2019 is hosted by the Freiburg Galaxy Project, the University of Freiburg, and de.NBI (the German Network for Bioinformatics Infrastructure). The event is organized by members of the host organizations and the larger Galaxy Community.
