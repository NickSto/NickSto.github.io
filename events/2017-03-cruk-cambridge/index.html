Introduction to Galaxy - Data Manipulation and Visualisation

Cambridge, UK, for first time users

  • Date: 7 March 2017
  • Location: Cancer Research UK Cambridge Institute, Cambridge, United Kingdom
  • Contact: Anne Pajon, Jing Su

See (external) url

A Galaxy introduction course covering basic functions, simple data manipulation using use cases and examples and visualisation mostly targeted at first time users.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NSF, NHGRI, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
