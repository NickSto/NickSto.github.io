BioHackathon 2018 Paris

Advance the development of an open-source infrastructure for data integration

Contact: Bérénice Batut, Hervé Ménager

12 November 2018

Galaxy will be among the topics covered at BioHackathon 2018 Paris:

  • CWL support in Galaxy
  • Galaxy training material improvement and extension

Interested? Spots are available on a first come, first serve basis. The registration is free and covers the catering expenses for the first 80 registrations. Register now.
