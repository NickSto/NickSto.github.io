Galaxy @ Gateways 2019

Co-located with eScience 2019

  • Date: 23 September 2019 - 25 September 2019
  • Location: Gateways 2019, San Diego, California, United States
  • Contact: James Taylor, Dave Clements, Mo Heydarian
  • Find out more

There are several Galaxy related presentations at Gateways 2019:

Date When Topic Where Presenter
Tues Sept 24 8:30 Workshop on Research Objects 2019 (RO2019) Cockatoo Room (Part of E-Science) Frederik Coppens
9:00 Keynote - Galaxy: from Genomic Science Gateway to Global Community (Slides, Video) Kon Tiki Room James Taylor
4:15 Plenary Panel: Effective Checklists for Developers and Researchers to Gather Requirements for Science Gateways (Video) Kon Tiki Room Sandra Gesing, Marlon Pierce, Maytal Dahan, Shawn Rice, Kyle Chard, Mark Miller, Dave Clements

Find out more

Galaxy is also a Gateways 2019 Bronze Sponsor. Please drop by the Galaxy table during any break on Tuesday and Wednesday, and during the Reception and Poster Session on Tuesday evening. We are also available at other times too. Just ask Mo Heydarian or Dave Clements.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NSF, NHGRI, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
