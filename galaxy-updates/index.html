Galaxy Updates feature news of interest to the Galaxy community. They come out approximately monthly, and complement the Galaxy Development News Briefs, which accompany Galaxy releases and are aimed at those developing and deploying Galaxy. Galaxy Updates are more general, and are geared towards all of the Galaxy community.
