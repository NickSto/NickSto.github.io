CropGalaxy

Public server:CropGalaxy
Scope:Domain specific
Summary:Hosts tools for the RiceGalaxy, GOBii project, HTPG project and the Excellence in Breeding (EiB) platform, including genomic selection, marker selection, GWAS, imputation, file conversion, and cluster analysis.

Comments

  • Through the Excellence in Breeding Platform the CGIAR intends to modernize breeding programs targeting the developing world for greater impact on food and nutrition security, climate change adaptation and development.
