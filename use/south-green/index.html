South Green

Public server:South Green Galaxy
Scope:Domain specific
Summary:Genomic resource analysis of southern and Mediterranean plants.

Comments

  • "South Green Bioinformatics Platform (SGBP) is a bioinformatics platform applied to the genomic resource analysis of southern and Mediterranean plants."
  • Supports many custom tools and data sources.

User Support

    Quotas

    • 10 MB storage quota for anonymous users. Account creation is restricted to those with an active collaboration and that work on southern and Mediterranean plants.
