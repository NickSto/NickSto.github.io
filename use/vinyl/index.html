VINYL (Variant prIoritizatioN bY survivaL analysis)

Public server:VINYL on Laniakea
Scope:Tool Publishing
Summary:A flexible and fully automated system for the functional annotation and prioritization of genetic variants.

Comments

  • VINYL runs on the Laniakea research cloud.

User Support

  • VINYL Manual, including a tutorial.

Quotas
