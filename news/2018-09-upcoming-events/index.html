4 Big Galaxy Events Before 2018 is Done

eResearch Australasia, Translational Medicine, BioHackathon, European Galaxy Days

20 September 2018

It occurred to me while I was typing up the Galaxy Admin Training announcement that there were several other big and upcoming Galaxy events before the end of this year that you may want to look at. Here are 4 of them:

Galaxy @ eResearch Australia

  • 15-19 October, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
  • 4 presentations and workshops over 3 days.
  • Register

Bioinformatics for Translational Medicine using Galaxy: see it, do it, teach it!

  • 17-19 October, Amsterdam, the Netherlands
  • Register

Galaxy @ BioHackathon 2018

  • 12-16 November, Paris, France
  • Includes CWL Support in Galaxy and Galaxy training material improvement and extension
  • Register

European Galaxy Days

  • 19-20 November, Freiburg, Germany
  • Register

Plus more events around the world

       

In addition to these events, there are many others offered this year, and around the world (Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America) that are less than 3 days long (and are even sometimes in French). See the Galaxy Events Horizon for a complete listing.

We hope to see you somewhere!

Dave Clements
