GCCBOSC 2018 Videos are Online

Missed it? Now you can watch all 70+ talks from GCCBOSC 2018.

14 November 2018

We are pleased to announce that videos from GCCBOSC 2018 are now available online and are linked to from the conference web site. Talks from the GCC parts of the meeting are available on a Galaxy Vimeo channel, and talks from the BOSC parts are available on a BOSC YouTube channel (and joint keynotes are available on both).

There are over 70 presentations across both channels, showing every talk from the opening keynote to the closing keynote.

See you next year in Freiburg (Galaxy) and Basel (BOSC)!
